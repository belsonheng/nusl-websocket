Electronic resources for NUS alumni and personal external members

The following databases are now available for access by our alumni and personal external members within the NUS Libraries

  • ABI/INFORM Complete
    Comprehensive database of full text journals, working papers, trade publications, conference proceedings covering the business, management, finance, accounting, economics, public administration and more.
  • Academic Search Complete
    Multidisciplinary full text database with full text periodicals including peer reviewed journals. Offers indexing and abstracts for reports, monographs, conference proceedings, etc. Features searchable PDF content going back as far as 1887.
  • Business Source Premier
    Business research database that features full text and searchable cited references for top journals covering a variety of business disciplines accounting, finance, economics, marketing, management, management information systems and operations management.
  • EBSCOHost
    A single easy-to-use platform to access subscribed content from various publishers, covering numerous subjects including business, economics, political science, psychology and sociology. The following databases are accessible on this platform:
    • Bibliography of Asian Studies
    • CINAHL Plus with Full Text
    • Communication & Mass Media Complete
    • Historical Abstracts
    • Humanities Abstracts
    • Social Sciences Abstracts
  • Emerald Management
    Offers full text access to journals and abstracts and reviews from Computer Abstracts International Database, Computer & Communications Security Abstracts, International Civil Engineering Abstracts, and Emerald Management Reviews.
  • IEEE Xplore
    Provides full text access to IEEE transactions, journals, magazines, conference proceedings, standards and IEEE-Wiley e-books.
  • IngentaConnect
    Hosts scholarly books and journals from a range of different publishers. Provides free searching and browsing of articles from academic and professional publications.
  • JSTOR
    Online full text access to back issues of selected scholarly journals in history, economics, political science, demography, mathematics and other fields of the humanities and social sciences. Consult the online tables of contents for holdings, as coverage varies for each title.
  • OVIDSP
    The platforms aggregates scientific, technical, humanities, social science and medical journals. It covers specialised databases including:
    • EconLit
    • Geobase
    • International Pharmaceutical Abstracts
    • MEDLINE
    • PsycInfo; PsycArticles
    • Social Work Abstracts
  • Project MUSE
    A unique collaboration between libraries and publishers providing 100% full-text and user-friendly online access to over 380 high quality humanities and social sciences journals from over 60 scholarly publishers.
  • ProQuest
    Resource includes citations and full text articles in academic & professional disciplines, e.g., business, economics, gender studies, health, literature, management, political science; as well as news and general interest items. Search across multiple databases or select individually from those which NUS Library subscribes to, including:
    • Humanities Index
    • International Bibliography of the Social Sciences
    • Linguistics and Language Behavior Abstracts
    • MLA International Bibliography
    • ProQuest Dissertations & Theses Global
    • ProQuest Sociology
    • ProQuest Political Science
  • PsycInfo (via OVIDSP)
    Search for the largest resource devoted to peer-reviewed literature in psychology, behavioural science and mental health. Produced by the American Psychological Association.
  • Records of the Colonial Office, Commonwealth and Foreign and Commonwealth Offices, Empire Marketing Board, and related bodies
    Official documents related to the administration & government of the colonies and territories administered directly by the British over Malaya, Singapore, and the Borneo territories. First-time users may wish to read this guide that also contains the series of sources available in the library.
  • Sage
    Search Sage Journals Online for abstracts, table of contents and full text of all Sage published journals and subscribed titles. Sage Knowledge contains e-books and reference books published by Sage covering social sciences, education, communication and media, economics, gerontology and sexuality studies.
  • ScienceDirect
    Web database for scientific research that contains abstracts, tables of contents and full text of scholarly articles in business, economics, decision sciences, mathematics, statistics, computer science, social sciences and psychology.
  • Taylor & Francis Online
    Online resource of journals published by Taylor & Francis, Routledge, Psychology Press and Informa Healthcare. The Library has access to titles in the humanities, social sciences and applied sciences. Dynamic young titles in the arts, humanities, education, behavioural, social, physical and environmental sciences are also available in the Taylor & Francis Fresh Collection.
  • Web of Science
    One of the largest multidisciplinary index covering literature from sciences, social sciences, and arts and humanities. It indexes scholarly journals, books, conference proceedings, and patents. It offers the option to find the Hot and Highly Cited papers, the most impactful papers within a subject field.
  • Wiley Online Library
    Provides full text access to journals, reference works, books and databases published by Wiley in the Science disciplines. Full text access is limited to journals and reference works for which the Library has a subscription.
